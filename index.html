Why Airspar

A New Category
of Event Structure

The Nineteener is a rigid inflatable dome built for brand activation. Not adapted from camping gear or event tents. It deploys fast, ships anywhere, and the form factor is unlike anything else on a venue floor or in a plaza.

Speed of Deployment

Built-in autopump inflates the entire structure in under 15 minutes. One crew member, no rigging, no poles, no crew call. Be operational while others are still unloading freight.

True Portability

Under 100 lbs packed. Fits in an SUV. Flies as checked luggage. No anchoring required for indoor use. For outdoor installations, heavy-duty ground stakes and storm bridles are included. Goes where rigid structures and frame tents cannot.

Distinctive Form

A full-hemisphere dome 19 feet across and nearly 10 feet tall at center. The geometry is immediately recognizable and unlike any conventional event structure. No comparable rigid inflatable delivers this scale at this weight class.

360-Degree Brandable Surface

The outer skin is waterproof and sublimation-printable across the full exterior. Submit artwork at order time and the dome arrives with your campaign integrated into the structure itself, not attached to it.

Custom Branding

Your Campaign.
Baked In.

The Nineteener's outer skin is a waterproof sublimation-printable fabric. Full 360-degree coverage. No vinyls, no banner clips, no exposed hardware. Your brand is not applied to the structure. It is the structure.

The oculus is a central opening at the crown of the dome. Two interchangeable covers are included: a waterproof opaque panel and a mesh panel. Depending on weather and temperature you can use either, or leave the oculus open entirely. Both covers are also sublimation-printable.

Submit print-ready vector files or 300 dpi image files at time of order. Custom print adds 14 days to manufacturing lead time.
